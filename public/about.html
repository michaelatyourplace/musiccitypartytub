About Us

Get to know the party planners behind the magic

Who We Are

Music City Bachelorettes was born out of one big idea: to give every bride-to-be the Nashville send-off she deserves. We’re a team of local insiders, party planners, and celebration specialists who know this city like the back of our hand. From iconic Broadway honky-tonks to tucked-away brunch spots and spa retreats, we’ve experienced it all—and now we want to share it with you.

Our mission is to make your bachelorette weekend effortless and unforgettable. We take care of the planning, bookings, and behind-the-scenes work so you can focus on having fun with your bride tribe. Whether you’re visiting from out of town or you’re a local looking for something fresh and exciting, we tailor every experience to your style and vibe.

We believe every bachelorette bash should be a blend of laughs, luxury, and local flavor. Our curated experiences include private party tours, line-dancing lessons, rooftop dinners, glam squad sessions, and even surprise appearances if you're into a little bit of sparkle and sass. We work with trusted vendors and local businesses to make your trip seamless and stylish from beginning to end.

At Music City Bachelorettes, we’re more than just planners—we’re memory makers. Whether you're keeping it classy or going full-on wild, we’re here to bring your dream party to life. So let’s raise a glass (or two) to friendship, love, and the unforgettable nights that only Nashville can deliver!
